Understanding the 9:16 Aspect Ratio

The 9:16 format is the vertical standard for mobile content, designed specifically for phones and social media platforms.

What is 9:16?

The 9:16 aspect ratio represents the vertical format optimized for mobile devices and social media platforms. For every 9 units of width, there are 16 units of height, creating a tall, narrow rectangle perfect for phone screens.

Perfect For:

TikTok videos, Instagram Reels, mobile content, Stories, and any video designed for vertical viewing on smartphones and tablets.
