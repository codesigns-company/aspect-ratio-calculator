Understanding the 4:3 Aspect Ratio

The classic 4:3 format, also known as "fullscreen," was the standard for traditional displays and still has many practical uses.

What is 4:3?

The 4:3 aspect ratio represents the traditional "fullscreen" format used by older televisions and computer monitors. For every 4 units of width, there are 3 units of height, creating a more square-like rectangle.

Perfect For:

Retro video projects, classic photography, older presentations, and content designed for traditional displays. This format maintains the nostalgic feel of early digital media.
