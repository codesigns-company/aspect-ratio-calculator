Why Video Dimensions Are Important

=======

Why Video Dimensions Are Important

>>>>>>> homepage-new-design

A video's aspect ratio defines its shape and how it appears on different screens. The two dominant formats today are:

  • Horizontal (16:9): Wide rectangles ideal for televisions and computer screens
  • Vertical (9:16): Tall rectangles designed for mobile devices and social apps like TikTok

Getting the shape right ensures your video fills the screen properly. Our video ratio calculator helps you determine precise pixel dimensions for any project.

Aspect Ratios in Film Production

Aspect Ratios in Film Production

In filmmaking, aspect ratio choice can dramatically influence scene atmosphere. Wider formats like 21:9 create cinematic, expansive feelings.

This differs from the standard 16:9 used for most online content, which provides balanced, immersive experiences.

Changing Video Dimensions

=======

Changing Video Dimensions

>>>>>>> homepage-new-design

When your video doesn't match the target platform's requirements, you can fix it. Most video editing software like Premiere Pro or CapCut allows you to adjust aspect ratios when starting new projects.

When importing video into a different aspect ratio project, you have two options:

  • Scale to fit: Enlarges the video to fill the frame, potentially cutting off edges
  • Add letterboxing: Shows the complete video with black bars filling empty space

The smartest approach is to shoot your videos in the dimensions you plan to share them in.
